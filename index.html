About
HereNow is a simple PWA App which can easily be viewed for a web browser or installed from your browser onto your phones home screen for easy access. This app was built to get you the information you need, quickly for your current location.
Location
Privacy
Weather information is pulled from OpenWeatherMap API. HereNow does not track you or any of your data. We simply grab your GPS data to grab the correct weather for you.
